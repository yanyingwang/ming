5.11 並

Racket’s value is especially called 𰅰 in ming-lang.

Racket’s Multiple Values is especially called 並 in ming-lang.

5.11.1 𰅰，並，𡌶

🐘 􏸾、􏸽

  • 𰅰: simplifies for 值, which means value in modern chinese.

  • 並: simplifies for 竝 in ancient chinese, means two or more than two objects as a whole, especially means multiple values in ming-lang. (Not confuse with 并).

Examples:
> (並 1 2 3 4)

1

2

3

4

> (𡌶 􏿴 (􏸧 (並 1 2 3 4)))

'(1 2 3 4)
